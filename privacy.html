Privacy Policy for Markdown Bookmarks Toolbars Firefox Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Markdown Bookmarks extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

User-Created Content

  • Bookmark Data: The extension stores the markdown content you create, including bookmark titles and URLs.
  • Storage: All bookmark data is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs.

Website Interaction

  • DOM Access: The extension accesses and modifies the Document Object Model (DOM) of webpages you visit to display the bookmarks toolbar.
  • Page Structure Analysis: We analyze page elements to detect fixed headers for proper toolbar positioning.

Third-Party Services

  • Favicon Service: When displaying bookmarks, we fetch favicon images from Google's favicon service (https://www.google.com/s2/favicons). This means the domain names of your bookmarks are shared with Google's servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your bookmarks in a toolbar on webpages
  • To maintain your bookmark organization across browser sessions
  • To provide the core functionality of the extension

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to our servers.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Domain information is shared with Google's favicon service only to retrieve favicon images.

Data Security

  • All bookmark data is stored locally on your device.
  • We implement security measures like URL sanitization to protect against malicious content.
  • The extension uses Shadow DOM for isolation to prevent interference with webpage content.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can edit or delete your bookmark data at any time through the extension's editor.
  • You can temporarily hide the toolbar using the "Hide" button.
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension's documentation.
